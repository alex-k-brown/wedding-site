Our Story

  • The Beginning

  • The First "I Love You"

  • Courtship

  • Engaged

Alex and Melanie met in September of 2013. Alex was working with his friend T on his clothing line, Koben Jeans (Yes, these are the famous silk lined jeans). They were hosting open calls for models as part of their marketing campaign and Alex had posted a flyer on the bulletin board next to Melanie's mail box. Seriously, can you say fate? When she walked into the audition in the Harrison Fine Arts Center on BYU's campus, Alex immediately looked up from behind his laptop. He smiled and said hello.

They didn't start dating right away. They talked via email and only about the photo shoot and, as far as Melanie was concerned, Alex Brown was just 'the Koben guy.' After a few weeks of professional interactions Alex finally asked Melanie out to dinner. They went to Cubby's in Provo and talked about other things besides silk-lined jeans. A week later they went to The Pie in Salt Lake City and talked for hours. The more they got to know each other, the more they liked each other.

Three dates later Alex sat Melanie down for “the talk”. Melanie let him down easy and said she just wanted to be friends (she was confused). Alex's response? He kissed her. Apparently, “her mouth was saying one thing, but her body was saying another...”

Whether it was Alex's impressive kissing skills or some stronger cosmic pull, Melanie's mind had been changed.
